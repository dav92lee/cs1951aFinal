<h3>Our Summary Review </h3>
<p itemprop="reviewBody">
                                    "American izakaya" dishes include a Foo Burger (cooked on a 1,000-degree robata) and fried tofu with quail egg and enoki mushroom at this upscale Southeast restaurant and lounge where Asian-fusion menus have a touch of Mediterranean influence. A sleek space decked in vibrant reds features a dragon mural, tatami details, a glossy bar area and a Buddha-themed patio.
                                </p>
