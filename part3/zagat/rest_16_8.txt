<h3>Our Summary Review </h3>
<p itemprop="reviewBody">
                                    "In the tradition of Pink's in Los Angeles", this Planet Hollywood outpost of the La Brea "legend" serves "sensational chili dogs" and other "snappy" franks laden with "just about anything"; there's only "outdoor seating on the Strip" ("great for people-watching"), and if some think these wieners are "pricier than they should be", late-night noshers say they're "just the thing after a long evening at the tables."
                                </p>
