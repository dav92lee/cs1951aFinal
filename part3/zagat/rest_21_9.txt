<h3>Our Summary Review </h3>
<p itemprop="reviewBody">
                                    Adding to their roster of Vegas eateries (Carnevino, OTTO Enoteca Pizzeria, B&amp;B; Ristorante), Mario Batali and Joe Bastianich launched this high-end burger joint inside the Venetian that also serves heros, salads, shakes and a broad selection of suds. The restaurant is done up in the duo's signature orange and yellow and features a huge patio facing the Strip – perfect for people-watching.
                                </p>
