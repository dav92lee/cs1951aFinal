<h3>Our Summary Review </h3>
<p itemprop="reviewBody">
                                    Mario Batali and Joe Bastianich oversee this "energetic" spin-off of their Manhattan pizzeria/enoteca in the Venetian, where patrons praise the "delizioso" food and wine purveyed under a "trompe l'oeil sky" in a "faux" rendition of "St. Mark's Square"; "knowledgeable" service and "budget-conscious" tabs are also part of the package.
                                </p>
