<h3>Our Summary Review </h3>
<p itemprop="reviewBody">
                                    Like its NYC progenitor, this "consistent" steakhouse chain's Strip outpost is a "beef lover's delight" thanks to its "huge slabs" of "well-prepared" meat delivered in a "macho", "club"-like setting; though it could easily "dent your gambling bankroll", the "polished" service lends credence to the belief that S&amp;W; "never goes out of style."
                                </p>
