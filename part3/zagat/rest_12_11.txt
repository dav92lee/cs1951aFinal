<h3>Our Summary Review </h3>
<p itemprop="reviewBody">
                                    "Definitely not your neighborhood sushi joint", this Japanese-American in the Cosmopolitan – an outpost of the NYC original – gives other top-tier fish specialists a "run for their money" with its "wonderfully prepared" rolls, plus a range of cooked dishes; its wood-paneled space hosts quite the "scene", while "helpful" service and late hours help take the edge off of "expensive" tabs.
                                </p>
